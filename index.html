Sandeepani Scholarship Scheme

The Karnataka State Brahmin Development Board has come forward to assist the education of economically backward/underprivileged students the Brahmin community under the Sandeepani Scholarship Scheme 2020-21.

Our Vision

One of the basic objectives of the board structure is to assist the education of the economically backward Brahmin community students. Therefore, the eligible students who are economically backward in the Brahmin community, studying in government schools, colleges or any private institutions, in all classes and levels, Kannada or English and Sanskrit medium, educational and other fees, text books, electronic equipment and other study tools in order to enable them to continue their education and earn a living by handling expenses such as transportation,clothing etc., the Board has recommended to provide tuition / educational assistance under Sandeepani Scholarship Scheme to help students of Brahmin Community to get further education without any financial restrictions.

Eligibility

  1. A student is eligible for scholarship if he is studying in Government or any private institutions and studying in Kannada or English.
  2. The student must have an EWS Certificate in his / her own name that is issued to the economically backward classes.

Mode of Application

Students who are eligible to apply for scholarship under this scheme, must submit all the details online, in the prescribed application form.

*Applications are accepted only online.
