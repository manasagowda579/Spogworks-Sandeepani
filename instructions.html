Guidelines for applying to Sandeepani Scholarship

Students applying for Scholarship need to provide accurate and authenticated information as printed on their Documents.

Sandeepani Scheme offers scholarship for economically weaker Brahmin community students studying in the following courses:

S. no Course Course Details Scholarship Amount in Rs.
1. Primary Primary Classes from 1 to 7th 7,500
2. Secondary High School from 8th to 10th 10,000
3. PUC/Diploma PUC/Diploma courses 20,000
4. Degree Courses ಪದವಿ ತರಗತಿಗಳು(ಕಲೆ, ವಿಜ್ಞಾನ, ವಾಣಿಜ್ಯ, ಕೃಷಿ, ಅರಣ್ಯ ಶಾಸ್ತ್ರ ಮತ್ತು ವೇದ - ಸಂಸ್ಕೃತ ಪದವಿಗಳು
ಸೇರಿ ಎಲ್ಲಾ ಪದವಿ ಕೋರ್ಸ್ ಗಳು ಆದರೆ ಎಂಜಿನಿಯರಿಂಗ್ / ವೈದ್ಯಕೀಯ ಪದವಿ ಕೋರ್ಸ್ ಗಳನ್ನು ಹೊರತುಪಡಿಸಿ)		 25,000
5. Engineering/Medical Engineering/Medical Courses 35,000
6. Post Graduation Post Graduation Courses 35,000
7. PHD PHD; ಸಮುದಾಯದ ಪರಂಪರೆ - ಸಂಸ್ಕೃತಿ ;
ವೇದ , ಶಾಸ್ತ್ರ ಪುರಾಣ ಮುಂತಾದವುಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ಸಂಶೋಧನೆ ಕೈಗೊಂಡವರಿಗೆ, ಸಂಶೋಧಕರಿಗೆ ಆಧ್ಯತೆ 		40,000

Important Note:

  1. Students are advised to provide "Annual Family Income" as per the Income Certificate issued by the competent authority in their Scholarship application.
  2. Scan copy of bank passbook
  3. Photocopy of the EWS certificate.
  4. Previous year (2019-2020) markscard copy.
  5. Fee receipt of the current institution in which the student is studying.
  6. Copy of the first page of Bank passbook
  7. Scanned copy of APL/BPL card of the applicant's family(not compulsory)

If you do not have a fee receipt when filing the application, you will need to upload the document provided here.

  1. Click on the download button.
  2. Take a printout of the form and get it signed as mentioned in the form.
  3. Upload it while filling the form.
